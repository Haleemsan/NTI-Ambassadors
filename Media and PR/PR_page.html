PR team
The PR team at the National Telecommunications Institute coordinates with event speakers and manages data for key attendees. They maintain organized records and collaborate with other teams to exchange knowledge. Their role ensures effective communication and smooth event management

Team Responsibilities

Responding to Student Inquiries

Assisting students with questions regarding available tracks.

Archiving Team Data

Maintaining reports and documentation for future reference.

Managing Online Interviews

Scheduling and conducting interviews for potential volunteers.

Helping with Registration

Assisting students with any registration issues on the website.

Organizing Key Events

Planning and overseeing events to ensure smooth execution.
