About Us

We are the Ambassadors Team of the National Telecommunications Institute (NTI) in Qena. As a dedicated volunteer team, we strive to raise awareness about the diverse range of courses offered by NTI at its Qena branch. Our mission goes beyond mere promotion; we actively engage with university students, providing them with valuable assistance throughout the course application process. This includes offering guidance on course selection, answering any questions, and ensuring that students feel confident in their decisions. Additionally, we are committed to documenting these courses through high-quality photos and videos, capturing the essence of the learning experience and showcasing the skills and knowledge that students will acquire. By highlighting the unique opportunities available at NTI, we aim to inspire a new generation of learners to pursue their academic and professional goals with enthusiasm and determination.

Team Departments

MEDIA

The Media Team is a dedicated group focused on promoting educational programs and initiatives. They create engaging content for social media, including videos and photos, to raise awareness and attract university students to various courses and activities offered by the organization.

OC

The OC Team is a dedicated group responsible for organizing and managing key events and activities. They coordinate online interviews, assist students with inquiries regarding available tracks, and ensure smooth registration processes, all while documenting team efforts to enhance future initiatives.

HR

The HR Team plays a crucial role in monitoring and evaluating the performance of various teams. They focus on task completion, assess skill gaps, and actively motivate team members to improve overall productivity and engagement, ensuring a supportive work environment.

PR

The PR Team is responsible for managing communication and public relations to enhance the organization's image. They develop strategies for outreach, create engaging content, and maintain relationships with stakeholders, ensuring effective dissemination of information and fostering a positive reputation.

Our vision

Our team is dedicated to reaching as many students as possible to raise awareness about the diverse programs available. We create engaging content that highlights the benefits of these offerings and manage our social media platforms to maintain effective communication with our audience. Additionally, we organize and participate in events that connect us directly with students, providing them with essential information. By conveying the importance of these programs, we aim to empower students with valuable skills and knowledge that can lead to personal and professional growth. Ultimately, our goal is to contribute positively to the community by guiding students toward opportunities that can make a significant difference in their lives.
