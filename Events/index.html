Events

Opening Day of the New Season

The new season of the NTI team kicked off with a special event, where a detailed overview of the key courses and tasks that will be offered in the upcoming period was presented.

The process of registration for these courses was explained, along with the necessary steps to join. Additionally, the most important requirements for participation were highlighted to ensure the selection of the most suitable members and to maximize the benefit from the offered programs.

Startup Olympics Awards Ceremony at South Valley University 2024

The NTI Qena ambassadors and pioneers participated in organizing the Startup Olympics Awards Ceremony, under the patronage of Dr. Ayman Ashour, Minister of Higher Education and Scientific Research, and Dr. Ahmed Akawy, President of South Valley University.

The event was supervised by Dr. Badawy Shaahat and attended by prominent figures such as Dr. Abbas Mansour, former university president, and Dr. Mohamed Saeed, Vice President of Community Service and Environmental Development, along with deans, faculty members, and students.

Welcoming New Volunteers

The event began with speeches from Engineer Mahmoud Fikry and Dr. Hany, who introduced the NTI team, explained the concept behind it, and outlined the goals we aim to achieve this season.

Afterwards, all new members were welcomed, and their needs were discussed to understand how they could benefit from the available opportunities. Workshops were organized for each committee to clarify their key roles and responsibilities.
